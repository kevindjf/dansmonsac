Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Summary: DansMonSac respects your privacy. We only collect data necessary for the app to function and never share it with third parties.

1. Introduction

DansMonSac is a mobile application developed by Kappsmobile ("we", "our", "us") that helps students prepare their school bag according to their schedule.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Data Collected

We collect the following data:

  • Unique device identifier: An anonymous identifier automatically generated to associate your data with your device.
  • Course data: The names of courses you create.
  • School supplies: The supplies associated with each course.
  • Schedule: The times and days of courses you configure.
  • Notification preferences: The time at which you want to receive reminders.

3. Use of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your schedule and supplies
  • Send reminder notifications to prepare your bag
  • Synchronize your data between your devices (if you use the sharing feature)
  • Allow schedule sharing with other users via a code

4. Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers, a hosting service compliant with modern security standards.

Data is also stored locally on your device to allow offline use.

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data access restricted to your device only
  • No passwords stored (device-based authentication)

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your data.

Your data may only be shared in the following cases:

  • Voluntary sharing: When you generate a sharing code to share your schedule with another user.
  • Legal obligation: If required by law.

6. App Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you bag preparation reminders.
  • Camera (optional): To scan sharing QR codes.
  • Internet access: To synchronize your data with the server.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the application. If you uninstall the app, local data is automatically deleted.

To delete your data from the server, please contact us at the address below.

8. User Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Children

DansMonSac is intended for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

Parents or guardians may contact us with any questions regarding their children's data.

10. Changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.
